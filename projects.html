My Projects

Tanks At War

Tanks At War is the name of my final project for the CS50's Introduction to Computer Science from Harvard University, is a game developed by myself using Lua and Love2d. All music, sound effect, and image files were made by myself. I am a musician so I know how to compose music and make sound effects, I used Ableton Live 11 and the Arturia midi keyboard to make the 3 music tracks and the 4 sound effects of the game.
